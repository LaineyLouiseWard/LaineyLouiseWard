\headerbox{Methods}{name=methods,column=0,below=introduction,span=2}{

We evaluate ECMWF subseasonal and seasonal reforecasts using probabilistic and deterministic metrics, including Fair CRPSS [4] and ACC, for 2\,m temperature at weekly lead times. CRPSS is computed after leave-one-year-out (LOYO) mean bias correction to isolate forecast skill from systematic model error.
