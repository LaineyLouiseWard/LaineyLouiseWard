\headerbox{Methods}{name=methods,column=0,below=introduction,span=2}{

As a first step towards D2F prediction, we verify 2\,m temperature forecasts at \textbf{2--6 week} lead times, stratified by season (DJF, JJA) and lead. Mean bias is removed by \textbf{\textcolor{corrblue}{leave-one-year-out}} correction, stratified by season and lead time. We also compare ERA5 against Met~\'Eireann observations to understand our reference's own biases. \textbf{Rank histograms} show whether the ensemble is reliably spread; \textbf{Fair CRPSS} [4] measures probabilistic skill relative to climatology.
\vspace{0.5em}
}
